About Me

At the age of 33 I can say I have already changed the direction of my career three times. With a background in education and most recently financial services, I have decided to move toward the direction of coding.

What ignited my interest in coding is my love for video games. A badly coded game can completely ruin your immersion. Bugs can completely destroy your experience whether it be unresponsive combat or constantly getting stuck in corners when exploring an open sandbox environment.

I love the idea of creating something out of nothing. Being able to turn an idea into something tangible. Coding is the future and I want to be a part of it. My ultimate dream is to someday program video games and help bring joy and entertainment to millions through flawlessly coded games.
